Ebadi Vertical Shaft Impact Crusher

The Ebadi Vertical Shaft Impact Crusher is designed to deliver superior results in tertiary and quaternary crushing applications. Our VSI crusher combines innovative technology with robust construction to provide optimal performance with minimal maintenance requirements.

Our flagship Ebadi Vertical Shaft Impact Crusher delivers exceptional performance for operations of all sizes. Its advanced rotor design ensures excellent particle shape and consistent gradation, making it ideal for a wide range of applications in the mining and construction industries.

Key Features:

  • Adjustable cascade/rock shelf system for optimal crushing results
  • Easy access for maintenance and parts replacement
  • Wear-resistant components for extended operational life
  • Advanced automation with touch-screen operator interface
  • Vibration monitoring and automatic shutdown protection
