Innovative VSI Crushing Technology for Superior Performance

Ebadi Vertical Shaft Impact Crusher Technology

The Ebadi Vertical Shaft Impact Crusher represents the pinnacle of crushing technology, designed to optimize the crushing process for maximum efficiency and product quality. We've perfected this technology over decades of research and development.

Our crusher uses rock-on-rock or rock-on-metal impact crushing principles, allowing for precise control over the final product shape, size distribution, and quality.

How VSI Technology Works

1

Feed Introduction

Material enters the crusher through a central feed tube and is directed into a high-speed rotor.

2

Acceleration

The rotor accelerates particles to velocities of 50-85 m/s, generating significant kinetic energy.

3

Impact Crushing

Particles impact either against the anvil ring (rock-on-metal) or against a curtain of cascading material (rock-on-rock).

4

Material Flow

The crushed material falls to the bottom of the chamber and is discharged, ready for screening or final use.

Technological Advantages

Superior Particle Shape

Our VSI technology produces consistently cubical particles with excellent shape factor, critical for high-quality concrete and asphalt applications.

Energy Efficiency

Advanced rotor design and optimized crushing chamber geometry minimize energy consumption while maintaining high throughput.

Versatile Operation

Adjustable operating parameters allow for processing a wide range of materials from soft limestone to hard granite and even recycled materials.

Reduced Wear Costs

Rock-on-rock crushing configuration significantly reduces wear costs compared to traditional crushing methods.
