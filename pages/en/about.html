Our Story

Ebadi Mining Machines Manufacturing was founded with a singular vision: to revolutionize the crushing equipment industry through innovation, quality, and customer-focused solutions.

Led by our founder and owner, Mehdi Ebadi, our company has grown from a small specialized workshop to a leading manufacturer of vertical shaft impact crushers that are recognized globally for their superior performance and reliability.

With decades of engineering experience and a deep understanding of customer needs across various industries, we continue to push the boundaries of what's possible in crushing technology.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide the highest quality vertical shaft impact crushers that maximize productivity and efficiency while minimizing operational costs for our clients.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in VSI crusher technology, setting industry standards for innovation, quality, and customer support.

Our Values

Integrity, innovation, excellence, and customer satisfaction guide everything we do at Ebadi Mining Machines Manufacturing.
